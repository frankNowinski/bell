Pennie is Punk

Pennie is Punk is a photo magazine about wearing denim in a punk way throughout all hours and during partaking in various activitie throughout the day. Pennie is a model an artist in New York City. All photos were taken in Union Square, Manhattan.

Life Begins

This project involved discovering creative ways to wear denim. So for the first photo, I cut scrap of denim from a pair of old jeans to make a choker which emphasizes the material's versatility.

Welcome to the Jungle

The titles for each page are pretty random, and are a result of how the photos feel to me.
